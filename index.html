about us

Blue Poppy Tours & Treks is a leading Bhutanese tour company, creating tailor-made trips to this magical Himalayan destination. Founded in 2005 by Choki Dorji, a native of Eastern Bhutan, we are based in Thimphu (Bhutan) with a small sales office in London (UK). We chose the name Blue Poppy as it is the national flower of Bhutan!
